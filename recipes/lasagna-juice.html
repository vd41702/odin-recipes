Lasagna Juice

A juiced lasagna (before and after)

Have you ever wondered what would happen if you drank the water from the bottom of a tray of especially watery lasagna? If so, here you go

Ingredients:
  • Lasagna
  • A glass
  • A fine mesh strainer
Directions:
  1. Bake some lasagna, following the directions here
  2. Cut a slice of lasagna, and use the fine mesh strainer to strain the slice into a glass
  3. Seek professional help
