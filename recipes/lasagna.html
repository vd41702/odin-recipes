Ingredents:

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Cheese
  • 9 x 12 glass baking tray
  • 1 cup of water (Optional, but recommended if you intend to make lasagna juice)

Directions:

  1. Start with a layer of marinara sauce in the baking tray
  2. Add a layer of pasta
  3. Cover the pasta with marinara sauce, and then a layer of cheese
  4. Repeat steps 2 and 3 until the tray is full, then add some extra cheese on top
  5. IMPORTANT! If you are making lasagna juice, then you need to add the cup of water now
